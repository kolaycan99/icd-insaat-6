FEATURED PRODUCT

BoscoMix P152

The Future of Concrete, Smart Crystal Technology

Although concrete is a strong construction material, it inherently has micro-pores and cracks that are exposed to the destructive effects of water and external factors over time. BoscoMix P152, unlike traditional methods, does not just protect concrete from the outside; it transforms it into a living, self-healing structure from within.

Why BoscoMix P152?

BoscoMix P152 is formulated with Advanced Amorphous Additive technology that penetrates into the concrete. Upon contact with water, these smart molecules become active and proceed into the capillaries of the concrete to form crystalline structures.

Self-Healing
Self-Repair

Throughout the life of the concrete, it automatically closes micro static cracks up to 0.4 mm by crystallizing. This maximizes the life of the structure while minimizing maintenance costs.

Impenetrable Waterproofing
Waterproof

Crystals penetrating deep into the concrete permanently prevent the infiltration of water and harmful chemicals (chloride, sulfate, etc.).

Full Corrosion Protection
Anti-Corrosion

Prevents corrosion of reinforcements (rebar) inside the concrete, preserving the load-bearing strength of the structure.

Eco-Friendly and Durable
VOC Free

Contains no toxic substances, it is a perfect choice for LEED-certified green projects. It shows unshakable resistance against chemicals in the pH 3-11 range and high hydrostatic pressure.
